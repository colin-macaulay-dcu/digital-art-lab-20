The Digital Art Lab

Digital technology is always at the centre of the DAL. Sometimes you'll start with paint or clay and bring ideas into a digital tool. Sometimes it's purely digital. What matters is the spirit of experimentation — the willingness to try something, see what happens, and try again.

Everything here comes from real workshops with trainee and qualified teachers — experimenting together, taking chances, and discovering what genuinely works in a primary classroom.
