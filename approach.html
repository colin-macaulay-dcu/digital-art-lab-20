What the DAL — Stands For

Three beliefs shape everything we make — and four steps take every experiment from the workshop floor to your classroom.

Our Beliefs

🧪

Experimentation Over Perfection

The best learning happens when teachers feel free to try things without fear of failure. The process of experimenting is itself the point.

🎓

Teacher Empowerment

Digital empowerment isn't just about knowing how to use a tool. It's about having the confidence, curiosity, and creative purpose to make your own decisions.

📚

Grounded in the Curriculum

Everything in the DAL connects to the new Irish Primary Curriculum — particularly its emphasis on creativity, agency, inclusion, and contemporary competencies.

From Workshop to Your Classroom

Everything on this platform starts in a real workshop and ends in your hands.

01

We Experiment Together

Trainee and qualified teachers come into the DAL — a real creative studio — to try things out with both traditional art materials and digital approaches.

02

We Document Everything

Photos, videos, teacher reflections — every step captured. Not just the finished work but the thinking, the stumbles, and the unexpected discoveries.

03

We Turn It Into Resources

Workshop experiments shaped into classroom-ready materials — lesson ideas, tool guides, exemplars, and curriculum links — practical, adaptable, and free.

04

You Take It Further

You bring resources into your classroom, adapt them for your children, and share what you discover. Your feedback shapes what the DAL develops next.
